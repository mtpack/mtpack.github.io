Types of Packaging Materials

Corrugated Boxes

Corrugated boxes are a staple in packaging for good reason. They offer strong protection for items that are heavy or need extra care. This makes them perfect for shipping a wide range of products. Their design allows for versatility in accommodating different sizes and shapes. Plus, they're highly recyclable, making them an environmentally friendly choice.

Flexible Films

Flexible films stand out for their ability to wrap around lightweight and oddly shaped items snugly. They provide a critical moisture barrier, ensuring the contents stay dry and intact. This type of packaging significantly reduces weight and space, optimizing shipping efficiency.

Shrink Wraps

Shrink wraps excel in keeping multiple items together, simplifying handling and transport. They add a layer of tamper-evident protection, which is essential for maintaining product integrity. Besides, shrink wraps improve product visibility while protecting against dirt and dust.

Blister Packs

Blister packs from Packaging Company offer a clear view of the product within, which can boost customer trust. They're excellent for tamper resistance and deterring theft, important for small, valuable items. This packaging option combines security with aesthetic appeal.
