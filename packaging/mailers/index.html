Introduction to Custom Mailers

Why Choose Custom Mailers?

Custom mailers offer versatility with gusseted and non-gusseted sizes, catering to diverse shipping requirements. The self-sealing flap simplifies closure and boosts security during transit. These mailers boast weather- and tear-resistant properties, ensuring content protection.

Businesses benefit from the option for full-color, 2-sided printing on custom mailers, enhancing branding opportunities. Moreover, a wide range of quantities is available for bulk orders, accommodating various business sizes effectively.

Benefits of Custom Mailers for Businesses

  • Gusseted and non-gusseted sizes: cater to diverse shipping needs.

  • Self-sealing flap: ensures easy closure and enhanced security.

  • Weather- and tear-resistant properties: protect contents during transit.

  • Full-color, 2-sided printing: enhances branding opportunities.

  • Range of quantities available: suitable for bulk orders of different business sizes.

Custom mailers reduce storage space significantly compared to traditional boxes, making them an excellent choice for businesses. The lightweight nature of mailers contributes to lower shipping costs, providing a cost-effective solution for businesses of all sizes.

How Custom Mailers Enhance Brand Identity

Poly mailers offer a space-saving alternative to cardboard boxes, reducing storage requirements and costs. Their weather-resistant properties make them ideal for protecting shipments in various conditions. The dual printing feature allows businesses to effectively showcase their branding on every package, reinforcing brand identity with each delivery.

  • Space-saving design: reduces storage space compared to traditional boxes.

  • Lightweight nature: lowers shipping costs for businesses.
