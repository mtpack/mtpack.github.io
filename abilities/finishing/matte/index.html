Common Questions

Why use Matte Finishing on Packaging?

In today’s world, product packaging plays a major role in businesses. Most of the time, the creativity displayed with the packaging is the only way for business owners to stand-out in front of their competitors. There are so many packaging options nowadays that one can easily get confused but one classic look is a matte finish that adds a degree of refinement and professionalism to product packaging.

Continue Reading

What most people think about when it comes to product packaging are the durability and cost. However, it is the visual appearance that makes the biggest impact. Not many people would want to come close to a dull-looking packaging, regardless of how amazing the contents inside are. The chances are that when in childhood you went to a store with your mom, you also often opted for the flashiest packages in the market.

If you are thinking about how you can enhance the visual appeal of your packaging, then the best way is by choosing the right finishing. There are a number of different finishing options for product packaging, but in this article, we will talk about Matte. Continue reading to know more about the benefits of matte finishing and why you should use it.

 

WHAT IS MATTE FINISH?

Now that consumer preference has been shifted more towards the design of the packaging, it has become more important than ever for you to focus on the packaging. Fortunately, matte finish provides you with just the non-glossy, sleek and sophisticated look you would want from the packaging of your product.

You can think of matte as the new black. Not only is this packaging easy on the eyes, but also overall adds a unique touch to your packaging and makes it stand out on the shelves. Many packaging companies have started to offer matte finishing due to its growing demands.

If you too want to add simplicity and sophistication to your packaging, then matte finishing is something you cannot go wrong with.

 

ADVANTAGES OF USING MATTE FINISH

  • VISUAL APPEAL

One of the biggest benefits you get from using a matte finish is how it makes your product packaging look aesthetic. Considering how the majority of the packaging companies in the past were inclined towards durability, the visual appeal of the product packaging was frequently compromised. However, with a matte finish, this does not seem to be an issue anymore.

  • PRACTICALITY

Apart from being visually appealing, the matte finish has an abundance of practical benefits to offer as well. Although some people may say that this finish is less flashy, that is its whole purpose. This sophisticated product packaging finishing helps it stay less reflective to light in stores. It often happens that businesses spend thousands of dollars on their logos, only for the light reflecting on the packaging to block it.

  • ENHANCING DURABILITY

If you want to make your packaging more durable, then that is another reason for you to go for matte finishing. It is known to enhance the overall durability of the packaging and make it more resistant to wear and tear. You would not have to worry about your product packaging wearing out over time just because it has been on the shelf for too long (that is if the matte packaging allows it to be).

  • SELECTED AREAS

Another major advantage of matte finish is that you do not necessarily have to apply it on the whole package if you do not want to. It can in fact be applied to selected areas only. If you want to highlight a certain part of your product packaging such as the logo, then matte finishing can help you get the job done.

  • FRESH PRODUCT LOOK

A matte finish can make your product packaging look fresher and newer. This can help your customers make up their minds and compel them to try out your products. Therefore, if you have recently been struggling with sales, then matte finish might do a great job to help you grow.

It goes without saying that the visual appearance of your product packaging plays a major role nowadays in the success of any business. If you truly want to capture the attention of the customers, then using finishing techniques such as matte on your product packaging can make a major difference.
